
NOTE: 

1. This folder should hold hex files (.hex) for BRAM memory initialization. 
2. Files should be named as following (where i is template number): template_i.hex

As such multiple templates will be available for each number. I suggest the following: 

    template_0  ... template_9        ->      hold 10 templates for the number 0
    template_10 ... template_19       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 1
    template_20 ... template_29       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 2
    template_30 ... template_39       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 3
    template_40 ... template_49       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 4
    template_50 ... template_59       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 5
    template_60 ... template_69       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 6
    template_70 ... template_79       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 7
    template_80 ... template_89       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 8
    template_90 ... template_99       ->      hold 10 templates for the number 9
